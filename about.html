About Chorley Town Café

Located in the heart of Chorley at 36 Chapel Street, Chorley PR7 1BW, England, Chorley Town Café has been serving our community since 2010. Our mission is to provide a warm, welcoming space where you can enjoy exceptional coffee, delicious meals, and great company.

We pride ourselves on using locally sourced ingredients and delivering outstanding customer service. Whether you're stopping by for a quick breakfast or settling in for a relaxed lunch, our goal is to make you feel at home.
